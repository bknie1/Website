About

Who He Is

Proud member of the Oregon Trail generation, Brandon grew up in a small town in rural Connecticut.

He was enamored with technology at an early age. When he wasn't playing Commander Keen he tagged along to computer shows and local tech swap meets (re: piracy). Final Fantasy VII, VIII, and EverQuest inspired him to create his own games in RPG Maker.

In high school he learned Visual Basic, basic HTML, Flash, Adobe Photoshop. He also picked up digital and traditional photography. He was, and still is, an avid musician; guitar, bass, drums, synth, and more.

He took the long way in college: He started as an environmental engineer, then earned a Sociology B.A. with a concentration in Criminology, a Criminal Justice M.S., and finally a Computer Science M.S.

Brandon, first and foremost, loves design. Whether it's games, web development, the user experience, or an elegant code solution, he's looking for opportunities to be creative and make users happy. He has academically programmed for about three years.
