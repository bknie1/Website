About

Who He Is

Proud member of the Oregon Trail generation, Brandon grew up in a small town in rural Connecticut. Legend goes that he fixed a "smoking computer" at 5. Exaggerated? Probably. Cool? Definitely.

He was enamored with technology at an early age. When he wasn't playing Commander Keen he tagged along to computer shows and local tech swap meets (re: piracy). Final Fantasy VII, VIII, and EverQuest inspired him to create his own games in RPG Maker.

In high school he learned Visual Basic, basic HTML, Flash, Adobe Photoshop. He also picked up digital and traditional photography. He was, and still is, an avid hobbyist musician.

He took the long way in college: First, he was an environmental engineer, then a criminologist, and finally a software engineer. Hello, World!

What He Does

Brandon, first and foremost, loves design. Whether it's games, web development, the user experience, or an elegant code solution, he's looking for opportunities to be creative and make users happy. He has academically programmed for about three years.

Variety is the spice of life. Developing a game and need some 3D assets prototyped in Blender? Brandon enjoys wearing multiple hats.
